Top 10 Tips to Crack MHT-CET 2025

Published on: June 21, 2025 | Author: Viraj Ambhore

The MHT-CET exam is one of the most important entrance exams for students aspiring to pursue engineering, pharmacy, or agriculture courses in Maharashtra. With the right strategy and consistent efforts, you can score high in CET 2025. Here are 10 tips to help you prepare:

1. Know the Exam Pattern

Understand the latest syllabus and structure of the CET. The exam consists of questions from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology. Know the marks weightage and time limits.

2. Stick to Maharashtra State Board Books

Focus on Class 11 and 12 textbooks prescribed by the Maharashtra board. Almost 80% of questions are directly based on these books.

3. Practice Previous Year Papers

Solving last 5 years' papers gives you a good idea of the question types and improves speed and accuracy.

4. Take Mock Tests Regularly

Use online platforms to take mock CET tests. Analyze your performance and work on weak areas.

5. Make a Study Timetable

Divide your daily schedule for each subject. Include revision, practice, and breaks to stay consistent and avoid burnout.

6. Focus on High Weightage Topics

Give extra attention to important chapters like Kinematics, Thermodynamics, Organic Chemistry, and Calculus.

7. Stay Away from Distractions

Limit social media and gaming. Set a study environment that helps you concentrate better.

8. Revise Smartly

Make short notes, flashcards, and mind maps. Regular revision improves memory and recall.

9. Eat Healthy, Sleep Well

Your brain works best when you are physically healthy. Take proper rest and eat nutritious food.

10. Stay Positive

Don't panic. Everyone struggles. Stay calm and trust your preparation. Self-belief is the key to success.
