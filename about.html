DailyNews is a student-focused platform that shares useful blogs, exam preparation guides, and educational news.

Started in 2025 by Viraj Ambhore, our goal is to help learners access quality content without distractions. Our content is curated, easy to understand, and designed for students in India preparing for competitive exams like MHT-CET, NEET, and others.

We also offer a Refer & Earn program so students can earn while they learn!
